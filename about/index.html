About this site

This site is an open-source, client-side image inspection tool for anyone who needs to quickly check a photo's format, dimensions and EXIF metadata. All parsing happens locally in the browser; no image is collected or uploaded. The content is compiled by the editorial team from public technical specifications and official documentation (see the sources in the footer) and does not represent any vendor. Related product: 2345 Picture Viewer, an image viewer for Windows.

关于本站

本站是一个开源的纯前端图片检视小工具,面向需要快速查看照片格式、尺寸与 EXIF 元数据的用户。所有解析都在浏览器本地完成,不收集、不上传任何图片。内容由编辑部整理自公开技术规范与官方文档(见页脚来源),不代表任何厂商。工具相关产品:2345看图王(Windows 看图软件)。
